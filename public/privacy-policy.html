🚗 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 7, 2025

Simple Summary: We collect your accident information, evidence, and recordings to help you document car incidents. We store it securely, process it with AI for transcription, and use it only for your legal evidence needs. You own your data and can delete it anytime.

1. Who We Are

Data Controller: Car Crash Lawyer AI

We provide incident documentation services for car accidents, helping you collect and organize evidence for insurance and legal purposes.

2. What Data We Collect

When you use our service, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, phone number
  • Incident Details: Date, time, location (including what3words coordinates), accident description
  • Evidence Files:
    • Photos of accident scene and damage
    • Dashcam video footage
    • Voice recordings and witness statements
  • AI-Generated Content: Automated transcriptions and summaries of your recordings
  • Emergency Contacts: Phone numbers for recovery services and personal emergency contacts
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information (for security and service improvement)

3. Why We Collect Your Data (Legal Basis)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Your Consent: You explicitly agree to our data processing when you sign up
  • Contract Performance: We need your data to provide the incident documentation service you requested
  • Legitimate Interest: Improving our service and preventing fraud

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Create comprehensive incident reports
  • Transcribe audio recordings using AI (OpenAI Whisper)
  • Generate AI summaries of witness statements for clarity
  • Store evidence securely for your records
  • Contact emergency services when you request it
  • Provide location services via what3words
  • Send you your completed incident reports via email

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for marketing without explicit consent
  • Share your evidence with anyone except you (unless legally required)

5. Third-Party Services We Use

We work with trusted partners to provide our service:

OpenAI (Transcription)

  • What they do: Convert your audio recordings to text
  • Data sent: Audio files only (no personal identifiers)
  • Location: USA (Privacy Shield certified)
  • Privacy policy: openai.com/privacy

Supabase (Data Storage)

  • What they do: Securely store your data and files
  • Data sent: All account and incident data
  • Location: EU/UK servers available
  • Security: Encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

what3words (Location Services)

  • What they do: Convert GPS coordinates to 3-word addresses
  • Data sent: Location coordinates only
  • Location: UK-based
  • Privacy policy: what3words.com/privacy

6. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Access Control: Only you can access your incident reports
  • Secure Storage: Files stored in private, encrypted buckets
  • Authentication: Supabase Auth with secure session management
  • Regular Backups: Data backed up daily to prevent loss

7. How Long We Keep Your Data

Default Retention: 365 days (1 year) from creation

After this period, your data is automatically archived. You can:

  • Request early deletion at any time
  • Request extended retention if needed for legal proceedings

8. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all your personal data we hold

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data

Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

Request deletion of your data (we'll comply within 30 days)

Right to Restriction

Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances

Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a portable format (JSON/PDF)

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests

Right to Withdraw Consent

Remove your consent at any time (may limit service functionality)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@carcrashlawyerai.com

We'll respond within 30 days.

9. AI Listening Feature - Special Notice

Our AI Listening feature provides continuous audio recording for hostile situations:

  • What it does: Records audio continuously across multiple app pages
  • When to use: Only in confrontational or threatening situations
  • Legal basis: UK law permits one-party consent recording (you are a party to the conversation)
  • Data storage: Transcripts stored, audio chunks processed then deleted
  • Your control: You start and stop the feature manually

Important: While legal in the UK, use this feature responsibly and only when documenting evidence is necessary.
